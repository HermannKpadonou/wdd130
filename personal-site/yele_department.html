Departments

Operations

The role of operations is to manage everything operational, IT, and the functioning of all systems to satisfy the company's customers. She works jointly with the other entities of the company to deliver the specifications concerning the company's projects. It also ensures the maintenance, security, optimization and evolution of systems, using the most suitable and innovative technologies. She monitors the quality, performance and profitability of the services offered to the company's customers, partners and employees.

Trades & Sales

The role of trade and sales is to manage the company's distribution network, which includes agents, distributors, resellers and end customers. He is responsible for the promotion, sale and monitoring of the services offered by the company, adapting the offers to the needs and expectations of the market. It also ensures the commercial relationship with partners, the negotiation of contracts, the management of orders and payments, and the processing of complaints. It thus contributes to the loyalty, satisfaction and growth of the company's customers.

Corporate

The role of the corporate is to take care of everything that involves partnership with other companies and structured networks. He is responsible for developing and maintaining strategic relationships with key players in the sector, such as financial institutions, international organizations, professional associations or the media. He represents the company with these partners, and defends its interests and values. He also participates in the design and implementation of joint projects, which aim to strengthen the company's notoriety, credibility and competitiveness. It thus contributes to the creation of value and the realization of the company's vision.

Compliance

The role of compliance is to help us comply with the legal, regulatory and ethical standards that apply to our payment and collection service activity. It controls entry into relationships, KYC (Know Your Customer), and ensures compliance with the company's internal and external regulations to ensure its proper functioning, security and reputation.
