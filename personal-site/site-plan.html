Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the intranet site for Yelemoney employees is to provide a space for information on the presentation of the company and departments, redirect to the official website of the company and the company's internal regulations. The website will provide employees with access to: • Company Presentation • Presentation of the departments and their different heads • Redirect to the company's official website and other related links • Consult the company's internal regulations.

Audience

The target audience for Yelemoney's intranet site includes all employees of the company, as well as managers and human resources managers, who are looking for essential information about the company, departments, official website, and house rules. They live in Ivory Coast and they are between 25 and 60 years old and need quick and easy access to the information they need for their jobs. They mainly use laptops and mobile devices to access the intranet site.
