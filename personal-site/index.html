Yélé Money mission

Yélé Money's mission is to support companies in optimizing the management of their financial operations, by offering them a tailor-made, accessible and secure payment and collection service.

Yélé Money's vision is to become a major player in the digitalization of financial flows in African economies, particularly impacting players in the agricultural sector.
