Overview

Purpose

My purpose is to create an attractive website to promote whitewater my rafting companie and encourage visitors to book an excursion. I want to offer comprehensive information about the different rafting options, inspire adventure and facilitate online bookings.

Audience

My target audience is adventure tourism enthusiasts, who enjoy whitewater rafting in groups, whether with friends, family, or colleagues. They want to experience thrills, share moments of conviviality, and create unforgettable memories. They are looking for tailor-made offers, adapted to their level and their desires, which guarantee them security, team cohesion and ease of booking from their mobile.
