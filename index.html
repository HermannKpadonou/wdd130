Aboisso,Côte d'Ivoire

Côte d’Ivoire, also known as the Republic of Côte d’Ivoire, is a West African country with Yamoussoukro as its administrative capital and Abidjan as its economic capital. It is bordered by Mali, Burkina Faso, Ghana, Liberia, Guinea, and the Atlantic Ocean. Known for its ethnic diversity, the country has over 60 different ethnic groups. French is the official language, but many local languages are also spoken. The economy is primarily based on agriculture, particularly the production of cocoa and coffee.
